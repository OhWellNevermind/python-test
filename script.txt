Manhwa begins with a demonstration of a large hospital complex, on the street the night is accompanied by rain

The actions are transferred to one of the rooms of the complex 

A man dressed in a white robe and soft slippers is standing in the room

A guy is kneeling in front of him 

The guy prays to the elder to accept him as his disciple, explaining that he wants to be like him

The elder was the same elderly man in a white coat

He holds out his hand to the guy and tells him to get up
